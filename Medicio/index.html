    About Us

    Alphy Medical Annex has been in existence for more than a decade, having been established in the early 2010s to cater for the health needs of the workers at the then port town of Kisumu. Since then it has grown to become the referral hospital serving County, Sub-County, and private hospitals in more than 10 counties in the Western Kenya Region with a population of more than 5 million people

    Departments

    Alphy Medical Annex has departments with specialized units, each dedicated to diagnosing and treating specific medical conditions and patient groups. Cardiology addresses heart-related issues, Hepatology deals with liver and pancreas ailments, Neurology focuses on nervous system disorders, and Pediatrics specializes in children's healthcare. These departments are staffed with specialized healthcare professionals who provide expert care..

      Any disorders related to the heart and the cardiovascular system.

    • Neurology

    • Hepatology
